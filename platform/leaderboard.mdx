The leaderboard ranks all Theta Labs traders by total P&L. It runs in two separate modes — one for live trading and one for paper trading — so your performance in each is tracked independently.

Navigate to the **Social** page and use the mode toggle at the top to switch between them.

## Live vs paper leaderboard

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Live trading">
    The live leaderboard ranks traders by their combined realized and unrealized P&L across all Polymarket and Kalshi spot trades.

    **What counts toward your ranking:**
    - Realized P&L from closed positions on Polymarket and Kalshi
    - Unrealized P&L from open positions, valued at current market prices
    - All trades made through your connected Theta Labs account

    Rankings update in real time as prices move and trades are settled. Active traders who trade daily are also eligible for a share of the Kalshi and Polymarket builder program rebate pool, distributed to active users.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Paper trading">
    The paper leaderboard ranks traders by their P&L in the paper trading tournament, which covers options contracts and narrative baskets.

    **What counts toward your ranking:**
    - P&L from paper options trades (calls and puts)
    - P&L from narrative basket positions
    - Performance is measured against your starting virtual balance

    The paper leaderboard is used for the Theta Labs trading tournament. Top finishers earn prizes from the prize pool.

    <Note>
      The paper trading leaderboard uses a $1,000 starting virtual balance. No real capital is at risk. Your options and narrative positions are tracked separately from your live trading account.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Rankings

Each row in the leaderboard table shows the following metrics for a trader:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Rank** | Your position on the leaderboard, ordered by total P&L |
| **Trader** | Your display name or wallet address |
| **Volume** | Total dollar value of trades you've placed |
| **Total P&L** | Combined realized and unrealized profit and loss, in dollars |
| **Return** | Total P&L as a percentage of your total trading volume |

The table is paginated in groups of 10. Use the page controls at the bottom to navigate through all ranked traders.

## Finding yourself on the leaderboard

Scroll through the pages to locate your rank, or look for your display name in the **Trader** column. Your name is set from your Theta Labs account profile.

If you haven't made any trades yet in the selected mode, you won't appear on the leaderboard until your first trade is recorded.

## Medal tiers

The top three positions receive special styling to highlight the leading traders:

| Position | Medal |
|----------|-------|
| **1st place** | Gold — highlighted row with gold border and text |
| **2nd place** | Silver — highlighted row with silver border and text |
| **3rd place** | Bronze — highlighted row with bronze border and text |

All other positions use standard row styling without a medal highlight.
