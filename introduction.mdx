Theta Labs is a trading platform that brings together the two largest prediction market exchanges — **Polymarket** and **Kalshi** — in a single, unified interface. Trade outcomes on politics, economics, crypto, sports, and more without switching between platforms.

Beyond spot trading, Theta Labs introduces **options** on prediction markets: buy calls and puts to get leveraged exposure with defined risk, or sell contracts to collect premium. You can also trade **narrative baskets** — curated groups of related markets treated as a single tradeable instrument.

## What you can do

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with one click">
    Theta Labs uses embedded wallets — no MetaMask or Phantom required. Sign in with your email or social account and a wallet is created for you automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit USDC">
    Fund your account by depositing USDC on Polygon (for Polymarket trades) or USDC on Solana (for Kalshi trades). Transfers between platforms are available from your Portfolio page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find a market">
    Browse the unified market feed, search by keyword, or explore trending markets, biggest movers, and markets expiring soon — all filtered from Polymarket and Kalshi in one view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trade">
    Buy YES or NO shares on any market, trade options contracts (calls, puts, or sell premium), or take a position on a narrative basket.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key concepts

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **YES/NO share** | A binary outcome token worth $1 if the event resolves in that direction, $0 otherwise |
| **Options (paper)** | Call and put contracts on prediction market outcomes — currently paper trading with a $1,000 starting balance |
| **Narrative** | A curated basket of related markets traded as a single price |
| **Buying power** | Available USDC across your connected wallets, ready to deploy in trades |
| **P&L** | Profit and loss — unrealized (open positions) and realized (settled positions) |

<Note>
Options trading on Theta Labs is currently **paper trading** — you start with a $1,000 virtual balance and no real capital is at risk. This lets you practice strategies and get familiar with the interface before live options launch.
</Note>
