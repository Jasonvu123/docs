A Narrative is a curated basket of prediction markets linked by a shared theme. Instead of trading each market individually, you trade the collective probability of an entire story unfolding — in a single position.

<Note>
Official Narratives (labeled **Theta Labs**) are curated and managed by the Theta Labs team. Anyone can also create their own **Social Narratives** and share them with the community.
</Note>

## What narratives are

A Narrative bundles two or more related markets from Polymarket and Kalshi together, each assigned a percentage weight that determines how much influence it has on the basket's overall price.

For example, an "AI Regulation" narrative might include:
- "Will the EU AI Act be enforced by Q3?" (Polymarket) — 50% weight
- "Will Congress pass an AI bill this year?" (Kalshi) — 30% weight
- "Will a major AI company face a government fine?" (Polymarket) — 20% weight

You buy or sell the narrative as one instrument, and your P&L reflects how all of those markets move together.

## How the price works

The narrative's composite price is a **weighted average** of the YES prices of its constituent markets:

```
Composite price = Σ (market YES price × weight %)
```

A market with a 60% weight has twice the influence on the composite price as a market with a 30% weight.

The composite price updates in real time as constituent markets move. You can view individual market price lines alongside the composite in the chart on each narrative's detail page.

<Note>
Weights are set at narrative creation time and are fixed. You can see the exact weight of each constituent market on the narrative's detail page.
</Note>

## Browsing narratives

The Narratives page is divided into two sections:

- **Theta Labs Narratives** — Official baskets curated by the Theta Labs team, covering themes like elections, macroeconomics, crypto, and geopolitics
- **Social Narratives** — Community-created baskets submitted by any user

Each narrative card shows:
- The composite YES price (in cents)
- The 24-hour price change
- The number of constituent markets
- The combined volume across all markets
- Source badges indicating which platforms the markets come from

**Top Movers** in the sidebar highlights narratives with the largest 24-hour price swings.

## Buying a narrative position

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a narrative">
    Click any narrative card to view the detail page. You'll see the composite price chart, constituent markets with their individual prices and weights, and a trade panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the basket">
    Check each constituent market's current price, weight, and expiry. This shows you what you're actually buying exposure to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your share count">
    In the trade panel, enter the number of shares you want to buy. The panel shows the total cost at the current composite price.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the trade">
    Click **Buy**. Your paper trading balance is debited and your position is opened at the current composite price.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When you buy a narrative, your capital is allocated across the constituent markets according to the creator-defined weights. The number of shares per market is calculated as:

```
Shares in market = floor(allocated capital ÷ market price)
```

<Note>
Narrative trading uses the same **$1,000 paper trading balance** as options trading. No real money is at risk.
</Note>

## Selling a narrative position

To close your position or take profit:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the narrative">
    Navigate to the narrative's detail page, or click your open position in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter shares to sell">
    In the trade panel, switch to **Sell** and enter the number of shares. The panel shows the current composite price and your expected proceeds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Sell**. Proceeds are credited to your paper balance at the current composite price. Your realized P&L is recorded.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can close part of a position — you don't have to sell all your shares at once.

## Risk and resolution

Constituent markets resolve independently on their own schedules. Your final payout depends on which markets in the basket resolve in your favor:

- If **all markets** resolve correctly, you receive the maximum payout
- If **some markets** resolve incorrectly, you receive a partial payout proportional to the weights of the markets that resolved in your favor
- If **no markets** resolve correctly, your position expires worthless

This means narratives naturally diversify single-market surprise risk, but partial losses are possible.

## Tracking your positions

Your open narrative positions appear in the **Positions** panel in the sidebar when you're signed in. For each position you can see:

- The number of shares held
- The current composite price
- Unrealized P&L versus your average entry price

Click any position to navigate directly to the narrative's detail page and manage it.
