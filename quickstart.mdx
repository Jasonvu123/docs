Theta Labs aggregates Polymarket and Kalshi into a single interface. This guide walks you through signing in, funding your account, and placing your first trade.

<Note>
Options trading on Theta Labs is **paper trading** — you start with a $1,000 virtual balance and no real money is at risk.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to app.thetalabs.gg">
    Open [app.thetalabs.gg](https://app.thetalabs.gg) in your browser. You'll land on the dashboard once you're signed in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in">
    Click **Launch App** and sign in with your email or a social account (Google, Apple, etc.). Theta Labs uses [Privy](https://privy.io) for authentication — no MetaMask, Phantom, or external wallet required. A wallet is created for you automatically when you sign in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deposit USDC">
    Go to your **Portfolio** page and click **Deposit**. Two wallets are shown — an EVM wallet (Polygon) for Polymarket trades and a Solana wallet for Kalshi trades. Send USDC to whichever wallet matches the platform you want to trade on.

    - **Polymarket** → deposit USDC.e on **Polygon**
    - **Kalshi** → deposit USDC on **Solana**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find a market">
    From the dashboard, use the search bar or browse **Trending**, **Biggest Movers**, and **Ending Soon** to find a market. Markets cover politics, economics, crypto, sports, entertainment, and weather.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Place your first trade">
    Click any market to open its trade page. Select **Yes** or **No**, enter a dollar amount, and confirm the order. Your position appears in the positions panel on the right side of the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
